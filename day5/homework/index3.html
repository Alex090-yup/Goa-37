Air China is interested in flights to Georgia – the airline’s plans

The largest Chinese airline Air China is interested in starting direct passenger flights between Tbilisi and Urumqi from September 2024, the Georgian Civil Aviation Agency reported.
According to the Agency, consultations with the airline on the start of flights are ongoing. Air China is considering the possibility of operating flights between Tbilisi and Urumqi three times a week.


Georgian women's national football team reached the qualifying playoffs for Euro 2025


The Georgian women's football team, thanks to its victory in the final match of the group round of the European Championship qualifying stage over the Lithuanian team (1:0), will play in the qualifying playoffs.



Ivanishvili explained why Georgian Dream needs victory in the parliamentary elections


For the Georgian Dream, this is a decisive struggle to finally strengthen peace in Georgia, noted Bidzina Ivanishvili


The ruling party needs a convincing victory in the upcoming parliamentary elections to finally put an end to the war party, agents, radicalism, polarization and “liberal fascism” in Georgia, said the founder and honorary chairman of the Georgian Dream, Bidzina Ivanishvili, at an event dedicated to the start of the party’s election campaign and opening of a new central office.


On Tuesday, the opening of both the central office and the Georgian Dream election campaign took place. Georgia will hold parliamentary elections on October 26 that will determine who will govern the country for the next four years.
