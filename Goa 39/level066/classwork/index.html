As of January 2025, the Georgia national football team, led by coach Willy Sagnol and captain Guram Kashia, features key players like goalkeeper Giorgi Mamardashvili and winger Khvicha Kvaratskhelia.
The squad includes experienced defenders like Solomon Kvirkvelia and Lasha Dvali, with a mix of young talents competing in UEFA tournaments.
